Care Instructions

To ensure longevity of your organic banana fiber undergarments:

  • Hand wash or gentle machine wash
  • Use mild, eco-friendly detergent
  • Do not bleach or use harsh chemicals
  • Air dry only - avoid direct sunlight
  • Iron on low temperature if needed
  • Store in a cool, dry place

Proper Care Extends Lifespan

Following these instructions maintains natural properties, ensuring comfort and sustainability.
